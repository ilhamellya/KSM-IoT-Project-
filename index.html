Nadin Amizah

Singer & Songwriter

About

A mature early adult female musician who writes songs with exceptionally well-chosen words and profound meanings, packaged in the genres of pop, R&B, and traditional music. Her music is considered by many as an alternative to relieve negative emotions, thanks to lyrics that often motivate.

Top Song

Sorai is a celebration, narrating the love story of Nadin that failed in her previous relationship. Contrary to the usual sadness associated with a breakup, Nadin chooses to celebrate this time. The term "Sorai" is used by Nadin to commemorate her success in letting go, forgiving, and learning from a love story that couldn't be united.

Bertaut tells the story of the restlessness, confusion, and happiness experienced by Nadin during her coming-of-age period. It depicts a child pouring their heart out to their mother. Despite being tormented by the cruelty of the world, Nadin is grateful for having a mother like hers. This song is special due to the profound use of lyrics.

Rayuan Perempuan Gila tells the story of Nadin's experience of once being in the position of begging for love from someone who already considered her crazy, as well as the overwhelming feeling of unworthiness to be loved by someone else. It is the most beautiful song among many beautiful songs in Nadin's second album, titled "Untuk Dunia, Cinta, dan Kotornya."
